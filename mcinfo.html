Basic Game Information

Minecraft is an open-world sandbox game created by Mojang Studios, where players can build in and interact with a three-dimensional world made up entirely of blocks. Its a game with limitless potential and infinate possibilities.

Spin-off Games

  • Minecraft Dungeons - A dungeon-crawler RPG developed by Mojang Studios and Double Eleven and published by Xbox Game Studios, where a group of up to four players can travel and fight through randomly-generated levels, while trying to stay alive as long as possible.
  • Minecraft: Story Mode - A choose-your-own-adventure game developed by Mojang Studios and Telltale Games, consisting of thirteen episodes across two seasons, where the player makes choices for the main character Jesse, as they and their friends go on epic adventures and save the world.
  • Episodes one through five were also added to Netflix.
