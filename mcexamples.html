Content Examples

Since Minecraft is only limited by what players can do in-game, the conclusion for making content from Minecraft would be that the content is only limited by what players can do with - or add to - the game.

  • Skyblock - One of the most popular minigames on Minecraft servers, originally created by veterin player Noobcrew, where the player can expand their island and build off of the limited resources given to them.
  • Modding - Changing or adding to the code of the base game, where players can add all new items, biomes, enemes, structures, and even stories to the game. This is where the limitless potential of Minecraft really shines.
  • Minecraft Bedrock edition - for consoles and mobile - added Add-Ons to the in-game marketplace, so that Bedrock players can experience playing the game with mods.
